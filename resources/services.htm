Our Service

TV viewers simply choose the Internet-based VOD content they want from the iNetVOD menu on their TV.  Behind the scenes, their broadband connected media player device connects to the iNetVOD service to search for and access content from multiple content providers using a single Application Programming Interface (API).  The iNetVOD system in turn connects to various content providers’ systems to perform the requested tasks.  Once the proper access requirements have been fulfilled, the player device streams or downloads the desired content from the particular content provider for the TV viewer’s enjoyment.
