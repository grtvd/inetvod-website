iNetVOD is creating a unique way to give more control to TV viewers in accessing the variety of video-on-demand (VOD) content offered on the Internet.  Imagine having a menu of choices for a host of Internet based content right on your TV!   

iNetVOD service is a VOD system that provides broadband enabled media player devices with a single point of access to content provided by a multitude of Internet-based content providers. 

iNetVOD service has not yet publicly launched, but system testing has begun.  If you are a media player device company, a content provider, or just want to be informed when we launch, please Contact Us.
