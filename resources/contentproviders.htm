content providers

iNetVOD service allows content providers to maintain a direct-to-consumer experience of their Internet-based VOD content. This results from TV viewers seeing their branded products on the iNetVOD menu. Other benefits to content providers include:

  • Enhanced distribution through multiple media player devices.
  • Exposing their content to other providers' customers, thus increasing their own customer base.
  • Increasing their customer base through potential cross-marketing between iNetVOD and media player device companies.
