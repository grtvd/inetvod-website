Our Service

Consumers simply choose the video or audio content they want from the media player's menu.  Behind the scenes, their broadband connected media player device connects to iNetVOD's Open Video Platformsm service to search for and access content from multiple content providers using a single Application Programming Interface (API).  The service in turn connects to various content providers’ systems to perform the requested tasks.  Once the proper access requirements have been fulfilled, the player device streams or downloads the desired content from the particular content provider for the consumer’s enjoyment.
