iNetVOD Privacy Policy

Effective April 2, 2008

Your privacy is important to us. This Policy applies to all of the iNetVOD websites, such as StormMediaPlayer.com, StormMediaPlayer.net, StormMediaPlayer.org, StormMP.com, StormMP.net, StormMP.org, iNetVOD.com, iNetVOD.net, iNetVOD.org (“iNetVOD Sites”) and to related iNetVOD software applications, such as Storm Media Player for Windows Media Center Edition, collectively “iNetVOD Services”. “iNetVOD Services” are provided by iNetVOD, Inc. (“iNetVOD”).

The iNetVOD Services and Sites are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age.

Information you provide

When you register for an account on an iNetVOD Site, we ask for your personal information (such as your name, email address and an account password).  We also optionally allow you to provide your address and credit card information.  iNetVOD uses your credit card information for enhanced services, such as show rentals and purchases.  Upon your consent, your credit card and other personal information is sent to an indentified third party for fulfillment of a request initiated by you.  You are not required to provide address or credit card information; however, as a result some services may be unavailable to you.

Cookies

Cookies are small bits of information placed on the hard drive of your computer. The iNetVOD Sites use cookies to make your browsing experience more enjoyable. Most cookies only exist for the current browser session. Some cookies are permanently stored for easier account logon. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, the iNetVOD Sites require cookies to function.

Log Information

When you use iNetVOD Services, our servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit an iNetVOD Site. These server logs may include information such as your web request, Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify you.

User communications

When you send email or other communication to iNetVOD, we may retain those communications in order to process your inquiries, respond to your requests and improve our services.

Affiliated sites

We offer some of our services in connection with other web sites. iNetVOD is not responsible for the privacy practices of these Affiliated Sites or other websites operated by third parties that are linked to any of the iNetVOD Sites. You should check the privacy policy of the other web site to determine how it handles any personally identifiable information they collect from you.

Changes to this policy

Please note that this Privacy Policy may change from time to time.  Changes shall be effective upon posting the new Privacy Policy on any of the iNetVOD Sites.
